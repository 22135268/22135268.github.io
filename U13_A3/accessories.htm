Types of Accessories

A PopSocket is a collapsible, disc-shaped phone accessory that sticks to the back of a phone or case, providing a secure grip, acting as a stand for hands-free viewing, and helping manage headphone cords. It "pops" out to offer a handle for your fingers and "pops" flat when not in use, making phones easier to hold

Phone cases are protective covers for mobile devices designed to prevent damage from drops, scratches, and daily wear, while often adding style or extra functionality.

Wireless chargers use electromagnetic fields to transfer energy from a charging pad to a device without cables. By placing a Qi-compatible device on the pad, the transmitter coil in the pad creates a magnetic field that generates an electric current in the phone's receiver coil.

In-car phone accessories focus on hands-free safety, charging, and mounting, including dashboard/vent mounts (magnetic or cradle), fast-charging USB-C/cigarette lighter adapters, MagSafe wireless chargers, and cable organizers.
