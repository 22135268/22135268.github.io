The Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra

The Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra currently holds the crown for maximum total storage capacity at 2TB, combining 1TB internal storage with 1TB microSD expansion. But here's the thing—several phones tie for highest internal storage at 1TB, and each serves different needs. According to recent smartphone storage data, the highest storage capacity available on a phone currently is 1TB for internal storage, with some devices offering expandable options that push total capacity even higher.

The Red Magic 11 Pro

The RedMagic 11 Pro+ is a high-end gaming smartphone featuring the powerful Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 chip, a 6.85" 144Hz AMOLED display with an under-display camera, up to 24GB RAM, and a massive 7500mAh battery with 120W fast charging, alongside advanced liquid cooling, 520Hz shoulder triggers, and IPX8 water resistance for ultimate mobile gaming performance.

The Oppo Find X9 Pro

It was the Oppo A5 Pro 5G that held this top spot for a good while, but it only makes sense that the phone would eventually be supplanted by another device from Oppo, given how much investment the company has put into improving its battery tech. The Oppo Find X9 Pro isn’t just the company’s best battery phone to date; it’s also the best battery phone you’ll find anywhere right now. Scoring a near outlandish duration of 26 hours and 40 minutes in the PCMark battery test, the Find X9 Pro eclipses competitors such as the Galaxy S25 Ultra and the OnePlus 13.

The best overall phone depends on your priorities, but top contenders for late 2025/early 2026 include the Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra for its S Pen, AI features, and camera, the Apple iPhone 17 for general users due to its strong formula and value, and the Google Pixel 10 for excellent Android experience, cameras, and software support.
